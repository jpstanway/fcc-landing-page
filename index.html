Conquer the skies.

Compact, with foldable wings, this lightweight but powerful quadcopter drone can shrink to the size of a smartphone, allowing you to take it anywhere with ease.

During flight, you can record 4K video, snap HDR photos, and make use of some handy features such as ActiveTrack, QuickShots, and SmartCapture.

  • 12MP CMOS sensor camera records 4K Ultra HD at 30fps or Full HD 1080p at 120 fps, and captures HDR photos
  • 3-axis stabilized gimbal enables in-flight camera movement to capture panoramas in vertical, horizontal, 180-degree, and sphere modes
  • 8GB of internal storage; integrated Micro SD slot lets you expand further
  • 21 minutes of flight time per charge thanks to high-density lithium batteries
  • Maximum flight speed of 42.5km/h (26.4 mph) and operating radius up to 4000m

The Mavic Air is a marvel of engineering that was built to go wherever your adventuring takes you. Enter the world of limitless exploration and discovery.
